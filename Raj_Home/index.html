Raj Medicity

Raj Medicity is one of Manipur’s largest multi-speciality Hospital located in the heart of the city, North A.O.C., Imphal west,Manipur, India. The Hospital was founded by Shri Haobijam Rajen Singh on 25th Feb, 2004 as Raj Polyclinic, it was renamed as Raj Medicity on 27th Jun 2014. The Hospital has been envisioned with the aim of bringing to Manipur the highest standards of medical care.
